research philosophy

How we work

i.

Learn first

Before building models, we aim to understand the clinical problem, existing literature, and practical limitations deeply.

ii.

Build carefully

Research should prioritize reproducibility, transparency, and realistic evaluation over impressive benchmarks.

iii.

Think long term

Our goal is not to publish quickly, but to contribute meaningful work that could eventually benefit healthcare.

about

A research notebook
becoming a laboratory.

Mastishk was founded in 2026 as an independent undergraduate research initiative. The project began with a simple question —

"How can AI become genuinely useful in healthcare environments where resources are limited?"

Rather than rushing toward ambitious claims, Mastishk focuses on understanding existing research, identifying meaningful open problems, and building a strong technical foundation before pursuing original contributions.
